About Us

Established in 1995, Winsome Model Schools stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Ifako, Lagos, Nigeria. Officially registered with both the West African Examinations Council (WAEC) and the National Examinations Council (NECO), we are committed to delivering a world-class education that fosters academic rigor, critical thinking, and character development.

Our institution is deeply rooted in the values of integrity, innovation, and community service. With a legacy spanning over two decades, we have consistently produced graduates who excel in national examinations and go on to become leaders in various fields across Nigeria and beyond.

Vision: To nurture innovative leaders equipped to thrive in a global society.

Mission: To provide a transformative learning experience through quality teaching, modern facilities, and a holistic approach to education.

Academics

Curriculum

Our curriculum is meticulously designed to meet WAEC and NECO standards, covering a wide range of subjects including Mathematics, English Language, Sciences, Arts, and Vocational Studies. We emphasize critical thinking, problem-solving, and practical application of knowledge.

Teaching Methodology

We employ a student-centered approach, blending traditional teaching methods with modern technology. Interactive smartboards, project-based learning, and collaborative activities ensure students are engaged and motivated.

Academic Achievements

Consistently achieving a 98% pass rate in WAEC and NECO exams, our students have won numerous awards in national competitions, including the National Mathematics Olympiad and the Nigerian Science Fair.

Admissions

Admission Process

1. Obtain and submit the application form.
2. Attend the entrance examination.
3. Participate in an interview with the admissions panel.

Requirements

- Birth Certificate
- Previous School Records
- Two Passport Photographs
- Medical Report

Fees Structure

Tuition fees vary by level. Please contact the admissions office for the latest fee schedule. Scholarships and financial aid are available for qualifying students.
